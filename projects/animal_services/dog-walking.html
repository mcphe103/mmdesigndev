Dog Walking

Your dog’s favorite adventure

Regular dog walks are important for physical, mental and emotional well being. But with today’s busy schedules, it can be hard to find the time to give your dog the exercise he needs. That’s where we come in!

Our dog walking services are a great choice for dogs and puppies who:

  • Are alone all day.
  • Have excess energy to burn.
  • Require extra potty breaks and socialization.
  • Need to lose a few extra pounds.
  • Have special needs or medical conditions.
  • Have behavioral issues related to boredom (barking, chewing, etc.).

Under the watchful eye of a professional dog walker, your dog will enjoy fun, stimulating and safe adventures. Every walk is tailored to your dog’s specific needs, energy level and personality to ensure the best experience possible. At the end of the walk, we check water bowls, make sure they’re comfortable and leave them ready for a good nap. That means you’ll come home to a calmer, more contented dog at the end of the day. All of our dog walks are completely private. We do not currently offer pack walks.

*All pets must have record of up to date Rabies vaccine or titer testing proving antibodies in accordance with city ordinance.
