Why Choose Us?

Orlando Dog Days Pet Sitting caters to pets who do not board well, the reactive or senior dog, the shy cat, and caged pets or fish. Many of our happy clients are dogs who have had unpleasant encounters with other dogs, or those who are not used to being kenneled. We reduce your pet’s stress by providing care in your home where they are relaxed and comfortable.

Satisfaction Guarantee

We are always prepared for your pet’s needs and ensure that they will receive the best care and love. Their happiness and care is our top priority and we strive to provide a service we would want our own pets to receive.

If you find yourself unsatisfied with your booked service, please email us at info@kmvandfriends.com, or call 701-969-0127 so we can make it right!
