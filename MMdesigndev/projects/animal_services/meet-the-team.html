About Me

Brandon Michael Lewis

Some information about me

My name is Brandon. Myself and Bella make up Orlando Dog Days. We have been in business since 2010.

I'm a freelancer helping others learn some martial arts and working towards helping others with pet sitting. My home has always been filled with pets. Cats, dogs, lizards inside.


Amy has a degree in Veterinary Technology. At a very young age, she showed gentleness and great affection for the animals in our home. Her home is also filled with pets

It's this love of animals that has led to us offering our services as pet sitters. We care about animals, We're knowledgeable about them and we know how comfortable we am when someone we trust comes in and cares for our animals when we're gone. Living in a rural area, we know how hard it is sometimes to get someone to care for horses, cattle, goats, chickens and other farm animals. We serve owners of farm animals and pets in and around the Fargo/Moorhead area and servicing areas around Casselton, Kindred, Leonard and Wheatland.
