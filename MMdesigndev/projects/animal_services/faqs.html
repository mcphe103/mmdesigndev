FAQ's

Why should I use a Orlando Dog Days?

Your pet will remain in his or her familiar, secure environment and follow his or her customary diet and exercise routine. Your pet will not be exposed to illness of other animals. Most importantly, your pet receives lots of love and personal attention while you are away. Orlando Dog Days sitters are employees who have passed a background check and are under the bonding and liability insurance. We are loving, dependable and trustworthy!

What do you do on a visit?

Our pet sitting visits include: Feeding per your instructions, fresh water, dogs walked, love and play time, brushing, litter box cleaned, bird cage cleaned, medication given if needed, mail and newspaper brought in, plants watered, lights on and off.

If my pet has an “accident”, do you clean it up?

Yes, we will clean up after your pets to the best of our ability. If there are always accidents, we will ask you to book an additional visit in the day. We will do a walk through to check for accidents.
